AI vs. The Cloud: Navigating the Hype and Reality

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Cloud Computing are two of the most transformative technologies of our time, yet they are often misunderstood and misrepresented. AI, with its promise of intelligent automation and predictive analytics, is surrounded by a mix of excitement and skepticism. On the other hand, Cloud Computing, a more mature technology, has become a backbone for many businesses, providing scalable and flexible IT resources.
